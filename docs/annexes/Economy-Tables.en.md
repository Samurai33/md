## Economic Flow Control Mechanisms

### Money Supply Regulation
- **Cash Injections**: New player starting money (M$5,000)
- **Subsidies**: Government jobs guaranteed minimum wage (M$1,500/hour)
- **Event Rewards**: Special events with cash prizes (M$5,000-50,000)

### Money Removal Mechanisms
- **Property Seizure**: Unpaid taxes result in property seizure after 4 weeks
- **Vehicle Impound**: Unpaid vehicle taxes result in impound after 3 weeks
- **Luxury Tax**: 15% on items valued over M$100,000
- **Death Tax**: 5% of cash on hand lost on death (maximum M$5,000)
- **Server Fees**: 2% transaction fee on all marketplace sales

### Economic Monitoring Tools
- Real-time economic dashboard for administrators
- Weekly economic reports and adjustments
- Dynamic pricing system for essential goods
- Automatic inflation control measures
- Anti-money laundering detection algorithms
